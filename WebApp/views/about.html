How We Got Started


The motivation for Automated Twitter Manager (ATM) came to one of our group members while he was working in a scooter shop. One of his tasks was making sure that the social media for the website was regularly updated, a task that frequently interrupted his other tasks. "Why do I have to stop what I'm doing every hour," he thought, "just to post something from a list on my computer? Isn't there a way to automate posting to social media sites such as Twitter?" Thus, ATM was created.

Using ATM

We feel that you shouldn't have to stop what you're doing just to post a preconceived idea. Instead, simply upload tweets ahead of time, and configure ATM to post them at regular intervals throughout the day.
